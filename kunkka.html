Kunkka - Dota 2 Hero

The Admiral of the Claddish navy, Kunkka commands the tides with power and precision.

Torrent

Summons a rising torrent that slows and damages enemies after a short delay.

Tidebringer

Empowers Kunkka's sword, giving him a cleave attack that deals bonus damage.

X Marks the Spot

Marks a friendly or enemy hero's location, then returns them to it after a duration.

Ghostship

Sends a ghostship sailing through the battlefield, stunning and damaging enemies in its path while granting a buff to allies.
